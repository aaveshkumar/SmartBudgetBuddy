## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit the Homepage**: Navigate to the ConnectWith9 portal homepage
2. **Click "Sign Up"**: Select your account type:
   - **Job Seeker**: If you're looking for employment opportunities
   - **Employer**: If you're hiring and posting job positions
3. **Fill Registration Form**:
   - Enter your full name
   - Provide a valid email address
   - Create a strong password (recommended: mix of letters, numbers, and symbols)
   - Confirm your password
4. **Accept Terms**: Check the agreement to continue
5. **Submit**: Click the registration button

Your account will be created and ready to use immediately.

### Logging In

1. Go to the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Enter your password
4. Click "Log In"
5. You'll be redirected to your dashboard

**Note**: For security, we recommend logging out after each session, especially on shared computers.

### Password Reset

If you forget your password:

1. Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. Click "Send Reset Link"
4. Check your email for a password reset link
5. Click the link (it expires after a certain period)
6. Create a new password
7. Log in with your new password

## Job Seeker Guide

### Dashboard Overview

Your dashboard is your personal hub where you can:
- View application status
- Access your profile
- Manage saved jobs
- Check notifications
- Browse job recommendations

### Creating Your Professional Profile

A complete profile increases your chances of getting noticed by employers.

#### Profile Sections:

**1. Basic Information**
- Upload a professional profile picture
- Add your bio/summary (120-500 characters recommended)
- This is the first impression employers get of you

**2. Contact Information**
- Display email address (employers will contact you here)
- Phone number (optional but recommended)
- Add links to your portfolio or LinkedIn

**3. Work Experience**
- Click "Add Experience"
- Enter job title, company name, and dates
- Describe your responsibilities and achievements
- Add multiple positions to show career progression

**4. Education**
- Add your highest qualification
- Include university/institution name
- Add graduation year and field of study
- Include additional certifications or courses

**5. Skills**
- Add relevant skills (e.g., "Project Management", "Python", "Customer Service")
- You can add up to 20 skills
- Arrange them by relevance to jobs you're targeting
- Skills help employers find you in searches

**6. Certifications**
- Add professional certifications
- Include issuing organization and date
- Certificate scans/proofs are helpful but optional

**7. Languages**
- List languages you speak
- Mark your proficiency level (Basic, Intermediate, Advanced, Fluent)
- Multilingual candidates are highly attractive to global employers

**8. Projects/Portfolio**
- Showcase your work with project descriptions
- Add links to live projects or code repositories
- Include images or attachments to demonstrate your work
- This is especially valuable for designers, developers, and creative professionals

**9. Resume Upload**
- Upload your professional resume (PDF, DOC, DOCX format)
- Keep it up-to-date and aligned with your profile
- Employers may download this during the application review process

### Browsing and Applying for Jobs

**1. Find Jobs**
- Navigate to "Browse Jobs"
- Use filters to narrow down your search:
  - Job title or keywords
  - Location or remote options
  - Industry and job category
  - Salary range (if available)
  - Experience level required

**2. View Job Details**
Click on a job listing to see:
- Complete job description
- Required qualifications and skills
- Company information
- Application deadline
- Salary range (if provided)
- Any special requirements

**3. Apply for a Job**
- Click the "Apply Now" button
- Your profile information will be pre-filled
- Add a cover letter (optional but recommended - makes your application stand out)
- Click "Submit Application"
- You'll see a confirmation that your application was received

**Note**: You can apply for multiple jobs, but only apply if you meet the requirements and are genuinely interested.

### Tracking Application Status

After applying, track your applications in your dashboard:

- **Applied**: Your application was successfully submitted
- **Viewed**: The employer has opened your application
- **Shortlisted**: You've passed the initial screening
- **Selected**: The employer wants to connect with you
- **Rejected**: Unfortunately, you weren't selected for this position

You'll receive notifications when your application status changes.

### Communication with Employers

**When Selected for a Job:**
1. You'll receive a notification that you've been selected
2. The employer may initiate a chat with you
3. Click the "Messages" icon in the navbar to access the conversation
4. Share documents, images, or files through the chat
5. Communicate directly about interview schedules and next steps

**Contact Options:**
- **In-App Chat**: The secure messaging system within the portal
- **Email**: Click the email icon to send a direct email (opens your email client)
- **WhatsApp**: Click the WhatsApp icon to connect via messaging (no contact save needed)

### Managing Notifications

**Notification Types:**
- **Job Status Updates**: When an application status changes
- **New Job Matches**: Jobs matching your profile and interests
- **Messages**: When an employer sends you a message
- **System Announcements**: Important portal updates

**Accessing Notifications:**
1. Click the bell icon in the navbar
2. View your recent notifications in the dropdown
3. Click "View All Notifications" to see your full history
4. Mark notifications as read or archive them
5. Mark all as read with one click

## Employer Guide

### Dashboard Overview

Your employer dashboard provides:
- Posted jobs overview
- Application management
- Candidate search and screening
- Chat with selected candidates
- Performance analytics
- Job posting management

### Posting a Job

**Step 1: Create New Job Listing**
1. Click "Post a Job" or go to your dashboard
2. Click "Create New Job"

**Step 2: Job Details**
- **Job Title**: Clear, specific title (e.g., "Senior Python Developer", not just "Programmer")
- **Job Category**: Select the relevant industry
- **Company Information**: Pre-filled from your company profile
- **Job Description**: Detailed description of:
  - Primary responsibilities
  - Day-to-day tasks
  - Team structure and reporting lines
  - Growth opportunities
  - Company culture elements

**Step 3: Requirements**
- **Experience Level**: Entry, Mid, Senior, Executive
- **Required Skills**: Add specific skills job seekers should have
- **Qualifications**: Educational requirements
- **Required Languages**: If bilingual/multilingual skills are needed
- **Nice-to-Have Skills**: Optional but valued skills

**Step 4: Compensation & Details**
- **Salary Range**: Provide a range or "Competitive" if undecided
- **Job Type**: Full-time, Part-time, Contract, Freelance
- **Experience Required**: Number of years needed
- **Location**: City, country, or "Remote"
- **Application Deadline**: When you stop accepting applications

**Step 5: Review & Publish**
- Review all information for accuracy
- Click "Submit for Admin Approval"
- Wait for admin verification (typically within 24 hours)
- Your job will be published and visible to job seekers

**Note**: Jobs are reviewed by admins to ensure quality and prevent spam.

### Managing Job Applications

**1. View Applications**
- Go to your dashboard or specific job listing
- Click "View Applications"
- See all applications sorted by:
  - Most recent
  - Application status
  - Candidate rating

**2. Review Candidates**
- Click on a candidate's profile to see:
  - Their full professional profile
  - Education and experience
  - Skills and certifications
  - Resume (if uploaded)
  - Portfolio links
- View application timeline and interactions

**3. Application Actions**
- **View**: Opens the full candidate profile
- **Message**: Start a private conversation
- **Select**: Indicate you're interested in interviewing them
- **Reject**: Politely decline this candidate (they receive a notification)
- **Archive**: Hide application from your active list

### Candidate Search and Screening

**1. Search for Candidates**
- Navigate to "Search Candidates"
- Filter by:
  - Skills and expertise
  - Experience level and years
  - Location or availability
  - Education background
  - Languages spoken
  - Job titles (experience)

**2. Review Candidate Profiles**
- Browse filtered candidates
- Click on profiles to see detailed information
- Check portfolio links and projects
- Download and review resumes

**3. Reach Out**
- Click "Message" to start a conversation
- Introduce your company and opportunity
- Discuss roles that match their skills
- Build relationships for current and future openings

### Communication with Candidates

**One-on-One Chat**
1. Click the message icon next to a selected candidate
2. Type your message
3. Use formatting for emphasis
4. Share documents, contracts, or job descriptions

**File Sharing in Chat**
- Click the attachment icon
- Upload documents, images, PDFs, or spreadsheets
- Candidates receive files immediately
- Maximum file size: 10 MB per file

**Contact Methods:**
- **In-App Chat**: Secure, documented communication (recommended)
- **Email**: Direct email (opens your email client with pre-filled subject)
- **WhatsApp**: Quick messaging (no contact save required)

### Job Management

**Edit Job Postings**
1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click "Edit" on a job listing
3. Update any details
4. Click "Save Changes"
5. Resubmit for admin approval if major changes were made

**Close a Job**
1. Go to "My Jobs"
2. Click "Close Job" when you've hired someone
3. Applicants will see the position as closed
4. No new applications will be accepted

**View Job Analytics** (if available)
- Number of views
- Application count
- Candidate quality metrics
- Application sources

## Admin Guide

### Admin Dashboard

The admin panel is the central control hub for managing the entire portal.

### Dashboard Overview

Access key metrics:
- Total registered users (job seekers and employers)
- Active jobs posted
- Pending job approvals
- Application statistics
- System health and performance

### User Management

**1. View All Users**
- Navigate to "Manage Users" or "Candidates"
- See complete list of registered users
- Filter by user type:
  - Job Seekers
  - Employers
  - Admins

**2. User Information**
- Click on any user to see:
  - Registration date
  - Profile completion percentage
  - Recent activity
  - Application history (for job seekers)
  - Posted jobs (for employers)

**3. User Actions**
- **Suspend/Activate**: Disable or enable user accounts
- **Delete**: Permanently remove accounts (use with caution)
- **View Details**: Access complete user information
- **Send Message**: Communicate directly with users

**Note**: Users with suspended accounts cannot log in or apply for jobs.

### Job Moderation

**1. Pending Job Approvals**
- Navigate to "Job Approvals"
- View jobs awaiting verification
- Check for:
  - Appropriate content
  - Non-discriminatory language
  - Realistic requirements
  - Proper formatting

**2. Approve or Reject**
- Review job details thoroughly
- Click "Approve" to publish to the portal
- Click "Reject" with a reason to request revisions
- Employer receives notification with feedback

**3. Manage Published Jobs**
- View all active job postings
- Monitor job details and updates
- Remove jobs that violate community standards
- Track job performance (views, applications)

**4. Job Violations**
If a job violates guidelines:
- Note the specific issues
- Contact the employer with revision requests
- Remove harmful content immediately
- Repeat offenders may lose job posting privileges

### Report Management

**1. View Reports**
- Navigate to "Reports"
- See all user-submitted reports
- Filter by status:
  - Pending: Awaiting investigation
  - In Progress: Being reviewed
  - Resolved: Addressed
  - Dismissed: No action needed

**2. Report Details**
- Reporter identity (users can report anonymously)
- Reported item (job listing or user)
- Reason for report
- Additional comments
- Timestamp of report

**3. Investigation Process**
- Review reported content
- Determine if violation occurred
- Take appropriate action:
  - **Remove Content**: Delete inappropriate job/profile
  - **Warn User**: Send warning about violations
  - **Suspend**: Temporarily disable account
  - **Dismiss**: No violation found
- Document your decision with notes

**4. Follow-up**
- Send notification to reporter about action taken
- Communicate with reported user about violation (if applicable)
- Archive resolved reports

### System Notifications (Announcements)

**1. Create Announcements**
- Navigate to "System Announcements"
- Click "Create Announcement"
- Choose announcement type:
  - General: All users
  - Job Seekers Only
  - Employers Only
  - Specific Users

**2. Announcement Content**
- **Title**: Clear, attention-grabbing headline
- **Message**: Detailed announcement text
- **Urgency Level**: Normal, Important, Urgent
- **Schedule**: Publish immediately or schedule for later

**3. Examples of Announcements**
- Platform maintenance windows
- New feature launches
- Community guidelines reminders
- Success stories and testimonials
- Important date deadlines

**4. Track Notifications**
- See how many users viewed each announcement
- View engagement metrics
- Delete outdated announcements

### System Settings (if available)

**Platform Configuration**
- Email settings and notifications
- Password requirements
- Session timeouts
- File upload restrictions
- API keys and integrations

### Troubleshooting & Support

**Common Issues**
- User account problems
- Job posting rejections
- Payment/billing issues
- Technical platform errors

**Escalation**
- For complex issues, document thoroughly
- Contact technical support team
- Keep records for future reference

## Troubleshooting

### Account Issues

**Can't Log In**
- Verify email address is correct
- Check caps lock on password
- Try password reset if forgotten
- Clear browser cache and try again
- Contact support if issue persists

**Account Suspended**
- Check email for suspension notice
- Review community guidelines
- Contact admin support to appeal
- Provide explanation for violation (if applicable)

**Profile Not Complete**
- Fill out all required fields (marked with *)
- Add at least one section beyond basic info
- Upload profile picture for better engagement
- Save changes after each update

### Job Posting Issues

**Job Rejected by Admin**
- Check rejection reason provided
- Review community guidelines
- Revise job posting
- Resubmit for approval
- Contact support if unsure about requirements

**Applications Not Received**
- Verify job is still open
- Check job visibility settings
- Ensure job description is clear
- Check spam folder for notifications

### Message & Communication Issues

**Can't Send Messages**
- Verify recipient is active user
- Check your account status
- Ensure message isn't empty
- Try refreshing the page
- Report issue to support

**File Won't Upload**
- Check file size (max 10 MB)
- Verify file format is supported
- Try different file if corrupted
- Clear browser cache
- Use different browser if persistent

**Email Not Opening**
- Ensure email client is configured
- Use WhatsApp or in-app chat as alternative
- Check browser's email permissions
- Try again from different device

### Notifications Not Working

**Missing Notifications**
- Check notification preferences
- Verify you're logged in
- Refresh the page
- Clear browser cache
- Check email spam folder for email notifications

**Too Many Notifications**
- Adjust notification frequency settings
- Mute non-critical notification types
- Archive old notifications
- Use filters to organize

### Technical Issues

**Page Won't Load**
- Clear browser cache and cookies
- Try private/incognito browsing mode
- Disable browser extensions
- Try different browser
- Check internet connection

**Slow Performance**
- Close unused tabs
- Clear browser cache
- Disable extensions
- Try during off-peak hours
- Use wired internet if possible

**Browser Compatibility**
- Use latest version of major browsers
- Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge all supported
- Mobile browsers: Chrome, Safari, Firefox
- Enable JavaScript in browser settings

### Getting Help

**Contact Support**
- Use in-app support chat (if available)
- Email support team with:
  - Detailed problem description
  - Steps you took
  - Screenshots if applicable
  - Browser and device information
- Expected response time: 24-48 hours

**FAQ Resources**
- Check help section in portal
- Review this manual for common issues
- Search community forums
- Contact admin directly for urgent issues

## Tips for Success

### For Job Seekers

1. **Complete Your Profile**: A complete profile gets 5x more views
2. **Update Regularly**: Keep profile current with latest experience
3. **Use Keywords**: Include industry-relevant keywords employers search for
4. **Personalize Applications**: Write cover letters for positions you want most
5. **Respond Quickly**: Reply to employer messages within 24 hours
6. **Network**: Use employer messages to build professional relationships
7. **Stay Professional**: Remember everything is documented - be respectful

### For Employers

1. **Detailed Job Postings**: The more detailed, the better quality applications
2. **Prompt Responses**: Reply to candidates within 48 hours
3. **Clear Communication**: Be explicit about job requirements and next steps
4. **Professional Tone**: Represent your company well in messages
5. **Give Feedback**: Help candidates improve by providing interview feedback
6. **Post Regularly**: Active posting shows you're serious about hiring

### For Admins

1. **Regular Monitoring**: Check reports and approvals daily
2. **Clear Communication**: Provide detailed feedback on rejections
3. **Consistent Enforcement**: Apply guidelines uniformly to all users
4. **Security First**: Monitor for scams and inappropriate content
5. **User Support**: Help resolve disputes fairly and promptly
6. **Community Building**: Foster positive interactions and engagement
